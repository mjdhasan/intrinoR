### `getCompanySecurities`
***
***

### Description

 Returns Securities for the Company with the given `identifier` 

### Usage
```r
getCompanySecurities(identifier)
```

### Arguments
Argument      |Description
------------- |----------------
```identifier```     |     A Company identifier (Ticker, CIK, LEI, Intrinio ID)
### Value

 companies tibble
